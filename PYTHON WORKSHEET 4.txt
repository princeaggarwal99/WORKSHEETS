11.)  Interpreter:
      Translates program one statement at a time.Interpreters usually take less amount of time to analyze the source code. 
      However, the overall execution time is comparatively slower than compilers.
      Compiler:
      Scans the entire program and translates it as a whole into machine code.Compilers usually take a large amount of time to analyze the source code. 
      However, the overall execution time is comparatively faster than interpreters.
      
      python is interpreted language therefore interpreter is used in python.
